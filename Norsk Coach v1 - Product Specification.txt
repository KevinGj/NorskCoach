Norsk Coach v1 - Product Specification
Goal

Build a simple web/mobile application that helps an advanced Norwegian learner improve:

Pronunciation
Rhythm and intonation (prosody)
Speaking fluency
Listening comprehension

The target user is already conversational in Norwegian and does not need vocabulary drills, grammar exercises, or beginner lessons.

The primary focus is helping the user sound more natural and comfortable in spoken Norwegian.

Core Concept

A daily 15-minute session consisting of:

Shadowing (listen and imitate)
Conversation practice
Storytelling
Feedback and coaching

The application should feel more like a personal speech coach than a language-learning app.

User Profile

Example target user:

Lived in Norway for many years
Uses Norwegian in daily life
Reads Norwegian fluently
Can write Norwegian reasonably well
Limited opportunities to speak Norwegian regularly
Wants better pronunciation and more natural speech
Wants to improve understanding of fast native speakers
Design Principles
Avoid
Vocabulary flashcards
Grammar lessons
Multiple choice exercises
Duolingo-style gamification
Childish interfaces
Emphasize
Speaking
Listening
Repetition
Natural conversation
Confidence building
Norwegian speech melody
Daily Session Structure
Exercise 1: Shadowing (5 minutes)
Goal

Train pronunciation, rhythm, and sentence melody.

Flow
AI generates or selects a short spoken sentence.
Native-quality Norwegian voice reads it.
User listens.
User repeats.
Recording is analyzed.
Feedback is provided.
Example

AI says:

"Jeg skal på butikken etter jobb."

User repeats.

AI feedback:

Pronunciation: Good
Rhythm: Slightly English stress pattern
Suggested improvement:
Reduce emphasis on "skal"
Let the sentence flow more naturally
Metrics

Store:

Pronunciation score
Rhythm score
Fluency score
Exercise 2: Guided Conversation (5 minutes)
Goal

Practice spontaneous speaking.

Flow

Voice-only conversation.

AI acts as:

Neighbor
Colleague
Store employee
Journalist
Friend
Family member

Example:

Hvordan har dagen din vært?

Follow-up questions should be natural.

Conversation should remain entirely in Norwegian.

If the user switches to English:

Encourage return to Norwegian
Do not switch language
Exercise 3: Storytelling (3 minutes)
Goal

Develop longer-form speech.

AI presents a prompt.

Examples:

Fortell om gården din i Nordland.
Beskriv en fisketur du husker godt.
Hva liker du best med å bo i Norge?
Fortell om en utfordring du løste på jobb.

User speaks continuously for 1-3 minutes.

AI records and analyzes.

Exercise 4: Feedback (2 minutes)
Goal

Provide actionable coaching.

Output:

Strengths
Good vocabulary
Natural sentence structure
Strong confidence
Improvements
Long English-style vowels
Stress pattern on compound words
Speaking slightly too fast
One focus area for tomorrow

Example:

Focus on reducing stress on helper verbs such as "skal", "har", and "kan".

Pronunciation Coaching

This is the most important feature.

The application should attempt to identify:

Common English Influence

Examples:

Excessively hard R sounds
Flat intonation
Over-emphasis on content words
English vowel sounds
Norwegian Sounds

Track performance on:

y
u
ø
å
kj
skj
rs combinations

Not all users struggle with all sounds.

The system should learn over time.

Prosody Analysis (Important)

Beyond pronunciation.

Evaluate:

Rhythm

Does speech sound choppy?

Intonation

Does sentence melody resemble native Norwegian patterns?

Stress Placement

Are important words emphasized naturally?

Speech Flow

Are pauses natural?

This may initially be heuristic rather than linguistically perfect.

Even simple feedback is useful.

Personal Learning Model

The system should build a profile.

Example:

{
  "pronunciation_issues": [
    "y sound",
    "sentence stress"
  ],
  "strengths": [
    "vocabulary",
    "grammar"
  ],
  "common_patterns": [
    "English intonation",
    "over-pronounced helper verbs"
  ]
}

Future exercises should adapt based on observed weaknesses.

Progress Dashboard

Simple dashboard.

Metrics over time:

Minutes spoken
Consecutive days practiced
Pronunciation score
Rhythm score
Fluency score

Graphs are sufficient.

Avoid complex analytics.

V1 Success Criteria

After 30 days of use:

The user should report:

Increased confidence speaking Norwegian
Improved ability to understand native speakers
More natural rhythm and intonation
Less mental translation from English

Success is not measured by vocabulary acquisition.

Success is measured by sounding and feeling more natural in spoken Norwegian.
